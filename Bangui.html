The Bangui Wind Farm is a wind farm located in Bangui, Ilocos Norte, Philippines. It uses 20 units of 70-meter high Vestas V82 1.65 MW wind turbines, arranged in a single row stretching along a 9-kilometer shoreline of Bangui Bay, facing the South China Sea 12. The turbines are situated in a hilly area and are visible from a distance 3. The project was initiated by a $48 million grant, and Denmark delivered all the wind turbine generators for the 20 windmills 4. The wind farm has become a popular tourist attraction over the years, attracting millions of visitors 1. There is no entrance fee to this attraction 1. To get a view of the stunning windmill draw, it is best to visit the windmill farm during the daytime
