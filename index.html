Ilocos Norte is a province of the Philippines located in the Ilocos Region123. Its capital is Laoag City, which is in the northwest corner of Luzon Island12. The province is known for its beaches, waterfalls, and historical sites234. It is also the birthplace of former President Ferdinand E. Marcos, who led an authoritarian rule over the country
